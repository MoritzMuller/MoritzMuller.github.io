About Me

I am Moritz Müller and I am a Data Scientist working as senior researcher (postdoc) at Leiden University. My unique background in data science, sustainability, and public policy allows me to take a creative approach towards data challenges: I first try to understand the problem to be solved and then work backwards, using a wide array and creative combinations of data science methods. I have extensive experience in machine learning, advanced analytics, and natural language processing. I usually code in R or Python, and frequently use SQL for database operations. Born in Germany, I have been living in the Netherlands for almost a decade and completed a BSc in Liberal Arts and Sciences (Policy Science) at Leiden University, a MSc in Global Business & Sustainability at Erasmus University Rotterdam (RSM), and spent the subsequent years as a doctoral researcher at Leiden University's Institute of Public Administration completing a PhD dissertation in the field of computational social sciences (i.e., data science to help understand human behavior). Currently, I am a senior researcher (postdoc) at Leiden University. For more information, please have a look at my CV.
